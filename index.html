About me

I am currently a resident at the department of pediatrics at Hacettepe University, a tertiary referral hospital in Ankara, Turkey. I have been working with statistical analysis and predictive modeling of biomedical data for about two years. As a medical doctor, I like to produce solutions to problems in the field of medicine with artificial intelligence. These days, I am developing myself in special optimization algorithms, fuzzy logic, graph analysis, and time series algorithms in the analysis of biomedical information. Even more importantly, I am developing real-time analysis of stream biomedical data. In this way, we will have the chance to make an early diagnosis or interventions with anomaly detection algorithms in patients whose condition is more critical patients.
